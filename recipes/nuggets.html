Chicken Nuggets Recipe

Enjoy this tasty recipe for your own chicken nuggets.

Ingredients

  • 500g of chicken meat
  • 2 Eggs
  • some seasonig

Preparation

This dish needs approximately 30 minutes to prepare.

  1. Preheat the oven. Do not put the child in the oven.
  2. cut the chicken meat into appropriately sized pieces
  3. Dip and coat the chicken nuggets
  4. Bake nuggets for 25 minutes

Enjoy your meal!
